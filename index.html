Willy Winarko

Willy Putra Yudha Winarko, or often known as Willy Winarko is an Indonesian rapper and radio caster.

Willy Winarko started his career as a radio announcer, he then entered the world of music first in 2011. He released his first album entitled Suara Perjuangan released on September 3, 2016. He re-released his second album, entitled Kids which was released on July 20, 2018. He re-released a mixtape album entitled Audio Collage released on December 13, 2021.

In addition to being active in music, he also works as a broadcaster at Mustang FM in addition to being one of the musicians. Then he became a content creator, the honorable being The Rap Up, one of the contents discussing current issues and being a voice actor.
